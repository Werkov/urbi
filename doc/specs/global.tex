\section{Global}

\fixme{Lots of work to do here.}
\dfn{Global} is designed for the purpose of being global
namespace. Since \dfn{Global} is a prototype of \dfn{Object} and all
objects are an \dfn{Object}, all slots of \dfn{Global} are accessible from
anywhere.

\item \lstinline|cerr|\\
  A predefined stream for error messages.  Note that strings sent to
  \lstinline|cerr| are not escaped, contrary to regular streams (see
  \lstinline|cout| for instance).
\begin{urbiscript}[firstnumber=last]

[00015895:error] Message

[00015895:error] "quote"
\end{urbiscript}
